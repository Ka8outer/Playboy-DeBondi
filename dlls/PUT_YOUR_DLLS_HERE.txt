Put your three Bondi reader DLLs in THIS folder:

    BondiReader.DJVU.dll
    msvcr71.dll
    msvcp71.dll

These come from your own, legally purchased "Playboy: Cover to Cover -
The Complete Hugh Hefner Reader" hard drive or DVD set. They are NOT
included in this download and are not redistributed.

How to find them on your own set:

  1. Open the "Playboy: Cover to Cover" hard drive / DVD in File Explorer.
  2. Use the search box (top-right of the File Explorer window) to search
     for:  BondiReader.DJVU.dll
  3. When it appears, open the folder that contains it (right-click ->
     "Open file location"). The two other DLLs, msvcr71.dll and
     msvcp71.dll, usually sit in the same folder.
  4. Copy all three files into this "dlls" folder.

If msvcr71.dll or msvcp71.dll are not next to BondiReader.DJVU.dll, search
the drive for each of them by name and copy them here too. All three must
be in this folder for the converter to work.
